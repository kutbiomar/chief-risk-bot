# ChiefRiskBot Getting Started (Design Partner CIO)

_Last updated: 2026-04-17_

## 1) Sign in and create workspace

- Open `app.chiefriskbot.com`
- Sign in with your invited account
- Complete basic workspace setup (currency/timezone)

## 2) Upload your starting portfolio

- Go to **Onboarding**
- Upload holdings CSV (`ticker/identifier`, quantity, market value, classification fields)
- Confirm ingestion succeeded in **Positions**

## 3) Add documents

- Upload one or more custodian statements or capital-call/distribution files
- Open **Documents**
- Review extracted fields and wire instructions
- Approve validated records into portfolio

## 4) Run risk and briefing flow

- Open **Cockpit** and confirm AUM/downside/liquidity surfaces
- Generate a briefing in **Briefings**
- Publish and export PDF for committee circulation

## 5) Daily operating loop

- Review new documents
- Refresh risk + briefing
- Track liquidity runway and pending capital events

## Current MVP constraints

- Teammate invitation workflow is not yet self-serve in UI.
- Support requests should go through the design-partner support channel.
